Job Vacancy-1

Job Role ID : SE3265

Job Role Title : Software Engineer

Job Description : We are on the hunt for a talented and result driven Software Engineer to join our budding team. The ideal applicant will be enthusiastic about producing high-quality software solutions, possess a sound technical background, and be able to work well both independently and cooperatively in a fast-paced workplace with the right mix of technical professionalism and soft skills.

Salary Range : 120,000 - 140,000 AUD per Annum

Reports to : Software Systems Architect

Key responsibilities :

  • Engage with cross-functional teams to develop, create, and implement new features.
  • To achieve the objectives of the project, write code that is clean, efficient, and easy to maintain.
  • Collaborate in code reviews to guarantee code quality and compatibility to coding standards.
  • Investigate , diagnose and troubleshoot software difficulties, and give prompt resolutions.
  • Discover, assess, and deploy new technologies on a continuous basis for optimal development efficiency.

Key Skills requiremets:

  • Essential
    1. Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Computer Science, or a similar discipline.
    2. A minimum of 2 years of full-time work experience developing software.
    3. Full proficiency in at least one programming language, such as C++, Java, or Python.
    4. A strong grasp of algorithms, data structures, and concepts.
    5. Proven experience with web development frameworks, such as Angular, Flask, React, or Django, is required.
    6. Good problem-solving abilities, including the capacity to troubleshoot and debug problems.
    7. Outstanding interpersonal and communication abilities, with the capacity to work well in cross-functional teams.
    8. The capacity to perform well in a dynamic, fast-paced setting and handle several activities at once.
  • Preferable
    1. Working knowledge of cloud platforms like Google Cloud, Azure, or Amazon.
    2. Working in agile development teams and being acquainted with agile processes.
    3. Familiarity with CI/CD procedures and instruments.
    4. Database management expertise, including knowledge of relational and non-relational databases like PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and MySQL.
    5. Version Control Systems: Knowledge of version control programmes like Git.

Job Vacancy-2

Job Role ID : PRM268

Job Role Title : Project Manager - IT

Job Description : We are seeking a versatile IT Project Manager to join our team and manage the efficient handling of our IT projects. The ideal applicant will have extensive experience in project management, a thorough grasp of IT systems and technologies, and exceptional leadership and communication abilities.

Salary Range : 150,000 - 180,000 AUD per Annum

Reports to : General Manager - IT

Key responsibilities :

  • Manage the whole lifecycle of IT projects, from start to finish, ensuring that they are finished on schedule, within scope, and on budget.
  • Establish the project scope, goals, and deliverables in consultation with stakeholders and the other team members.
  • Create precise project plans, timetables, and budgets, and track progress towards important milestones and targets.
  • Ensure that all applicable policies, standards, and requirements are followed throughout the project's lifespan.
  • Coordinate and lead project teams, which include internal employees, external contractors, and consultants, to ensure successful cooperation and resource distribution.

Key Skills requirements:

  • Essential
    1. Skilled with project management approaches (e.g., Agile, Waterfall) with a minimum of 5 years of professional experience
    2. Technical aptitude and a thorough awareness of IT systems, technologies, and procedures related to the project area, as well as the ability to comprehend technical ideas and needs.
    3. The ability to manage and influence stakeholders at all levels, such as senior management, clients, and project sponsors.
    4. Versatility and flexibility to changing project needs, objectives, and restrictions.
    5. Strong verbal and written communication skills are required to successfully interact with stakeholders, team members, and clients.
    6. Risk handling and skilled in identifying, assessing, and mitigating project risks to reduce their impact on project execution and outcomes.
  • Preferable
    1. PMP (Project Management Professional) or other equivalent project management qualifications are advantageous.
    2. Industry knowledge refers to familiarity with the specific industry or sector in which the initiatives are being conducted.
    3. Experience with Agile approaches such as Scrum and Kanban, as well as Agile frameworks and practices.
    4. Knowledge of quality assurance procedures and techniques for ensuring project outputs satisfy quality standards and compliance needs.
    5. Fundamental grasp of business analysis methodologies and tools for gathering, analysing, and documenting project requirements.
